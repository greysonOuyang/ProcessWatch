# Disclaimer

ProcessWatch is a diagnostic utility, not an authoritative malware detector or energy measurement instrument.

- CPU, memory, disk I/O, ancestry, and orphan classifications are derived from available macOS process metadata and sampling intervals.
- The repo-harness and Codex labels are heuristic string and ancestry matches.
- Some system processes cannot be inspected by an ordinary user account.
- Sending `SIGTERM` can interrupt work or corrupt in-progress output. Confirm the process identity first.
- “Resource impact” and thermal descriptions are not replacements for Apple's Activity Monitor or professional profiling tools.

ProcessWatch is not affiliated with or endorsed by Apple, OpenAI, Codex, Bun, or repo-harness.
